What Is Interdude?

A platform that let's you prepare for real interviews with real people in a friendly environment. You will be paired with someone who wants to have an interview regarding the same topic and difficulty level, and in the same time slot. Log in, start the video call, answer the questions (or ask them!), and get graded!

Why Interdude?

Interview anxiety holds everyone back. You may be amazing, but if you cannot showcase yourself in front of the interviewer, your potential is wasted.

Interdude changes that for good. Sure, you can prep for questions, and practice with your friends, but the closest thing to a real interview is... an interview! With live video calling, mutual grading and result history, Interdude is the one stop solution to interview jitters.
